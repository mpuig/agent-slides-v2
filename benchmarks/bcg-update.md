# BCG Quarterly Update Brief

## Template
examples/bcg.pptx

## Brief
Create a 6-slide quarterly business update for an executive steering committee.
The client is a logistics company reporting Q1 2026 results: revenue up 12% YoY,
EBITDA margin expanded 200bps, but customer churn increased in the SMB segment.

Target audience: Executive Steering Committee
Objective: Communicate mixed Q1 results and proposed SMB retention initiative
Key recommendation: Invest EUR 1.5M in SMB customer success program

## Required content signals
- Q1 financial highlights (revenue, EBITDA, margin)
- SMB churn data and root cause
- Retention program proposal with ROI
- Next quarter priorities

## Expected slide count
5-7

## Layout variety requirement
At least 2 distinct layout types across content slides.
