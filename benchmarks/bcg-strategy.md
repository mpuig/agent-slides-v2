# BCG Strategy Brief

## Template
examples/bcg.pptx

## Brief
Create a 10-slide board strategy presentation for a European market entry.
The client is a US-based B2B SaaS company (EUR 50M ARR) evaluating organic entry,
acquisition, and joint venture paths into the EU mid-market.

Target audience: Board of Directors
Objective: Secure approval for EUR 4.2M organic entry investment
Key recommendation: Phased organic entry with channel acceleration

## Required content signals
- Market sizing (EUR 48B, 14% CAGR)
- Competitive landscape (US incumbents vs EU-native)
- Three entry options with trade-offs
- Financial projections (3-year NPV)
- Implementation roadmap (3 phases)
- Clear call to action

## Expected slide count
8-10

## Layout variety requirement
At least 3 distinct layout types across content slides.
